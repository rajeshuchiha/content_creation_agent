<h1>Man Arrested Over Online People Smuggling Posts in Birmingham</h1>
<p>A significant arrest has been made in Birmingham as part of a crackdown on illegal immigration. The National Crime Agency (NCA) has apprehended a 38-year-old man on suspicion of orchestrating illegal immigration through social media platforms.</p>
<p>The individual, who possesses dual British and Pakistani nationality, was detained in the Yardley area of Birmingham. He is alleged to have utilized various online channels to advertise and promote illicit boat crossings from North Africa to Europe, facilitating people smuggling services.</p>
